Welcome to Chameleon:

  • One player is randomly assigned as the Chameleon.

  • The Chameleon does not know the secret word; other players do.

  • Players take turns giving clues related to the secret word.

  • The Chameleon tries to blend in without knowing the secret word.

  • Players can call for a vote once per round to identify the Chameleon.

  • If the Chameleon is caught, the players win. If not, the Chameleon wins.

  • The Chameleon can guess the secret word once per round to win instantly.
